Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Home Assistant Google Assistant integration operated under the domain codesware.com handles information.

1. Purpose

This integration is a private, self-hosted application owned and operated by the domain owner. It allows Google Assistant to interact with a locally hosted instance of Home Assistant for personal, household use.

2. Information Collected

The integration does not collect, store, or transmit personal data outside of the owner’s own Home Assistant installation. Any information (such as device states or automation events) remains local and private to the owner’s environment.

3. Use of Information

Information processed by this integration is used solely for enabling communication between Google Assistant and the owner’s Home Assistant installation. It is not used for analytics, advertising, or shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

No personal data is stored by the codesware.com domain or its servers. All relevant data remains on the owner’s self-hosted Home Assistant instance.

5. Sharing of Information

No information is shared with or sold to any third parties. Data remains entirely within the owner’s household systems.

6. Security

The owner takes reasonable measures to protect the local Home Assistant installation. Since the integration is private and self-hosted, responsibility for system security lies with the owner of this domain and server.

7. Children’s Privacy

This integration is not directed to children and is intended only for use by the domain owner and authorized household members.

8. Changes to this Policy

The owner may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be published at https://codesware.com/privacy.html.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the owner at: admin@codesware.com.
